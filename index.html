Our Sweet Story

Since 1992, Sweet Dreams Bakery has been Chicago's beloved neighborhood bakery. Founded by Maria Gonzalez with her grandmother's secret recipes, we've been creating sweet memories for families across the city.

What started as a small family business has grown into Chicago's premier artisan bakery, but we've never forgotten our roots. Every cake, every loaf of bread, and every pastry is still made with the same love and attention to detail that made us who we are today.
